About Us

The Production Masters (TPM) is a Ghanaian Company incorporated in December 2018. We are a creative content creation and production company with a strong focus on Film-Making excellence, TV commercials, Promos and other Branded content. The Company is a Media Content Production House that deals with productions aimed at developing businesses and empowering our society. We have expert knowledge In traditional and new media. We work with industry professionals, individuals and organizations who have the passion for producing media content.
